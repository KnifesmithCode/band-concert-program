Dakota Valley Band Songs

Sleigh Ride by Leroy Anderson

"Sleigh Ride" is composed by Leroy Anderson. The composer had the original idea for the piece during a heat wave in July 1946 and finished the work in February 1948. The lyrics, about riding in a sleigh and other fun wintertime activities, were written by Mitchell Parish in 1950.

Candlelight Carol by John Rutter, arranged by Jay Dawson

"Candlelight Carol" was by the English choral composer and conductor John Rutter. The carol was written in 1984 and was first recorded by Rutter's own group, the Cambridge Singers.

Ornaments by Jon Bubbett

This song is a collection of favorite tunes from the holiday season.

Christmas Eve / Sarajevo 12/24 by Paul O’neill and Robert Kinkel

The piece reflects the drama of a single musician in the midst of war, reflecting on the power of music to bring hope.

Deck the Halls with Chips and Salsa by Ed Huckby

This is Deck the Halls with a bright Latin beat. We hope you enjoy!
